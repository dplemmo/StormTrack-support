Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 2025

Live StormTrack Navigator does not collect personally identifiable information from users. The app uses your device's location only to provide real-time navigation and local weather information. Location data is not stored or transmitted to our servers.

We do not collect your name, contact details, or usage behavior. Third-party services (such as map providers or weather APIs) may collect limited anonymous data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

If you contact us via email, your address will only be used for support purposes and will not be shared or sold.

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

For questions, contact us at: support@stormtracknavigator.com
